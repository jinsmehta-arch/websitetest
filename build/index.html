Obstetric expertise

Specialist private pregnancy care for birth and fetal wellbeing.

Dr Kotecha combines consultant-level obstetric experience with a calm, compassionate approach, helping families feel listened to, supported, informed and safe.

Dr Reena Kotecha is a Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with 22 years of clinical experience. Her private obstetric practice includes appointments at The Portland Hospital in London, supporting patients searching for a private obstetrician London, consultant-led maternity care and private pregnancy care.

Her obstetric specialisms include routine and complex antenatal care, twin pregnancies, maternal and fetal medicine, obstetric medicine and sonography.

Dr Kotecha's published HCA profile describes comprehensive maternity care with continuity, clear communication, informed decision making and individualised care planning throughout pregnancy.
