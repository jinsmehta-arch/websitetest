Obstetric expertise

Specialist care for pregnancy, birth and fetal wellbeing.

Dr Kotecha combines consultant-level obstetric experience with a calm, compassionate approach, helping families feel listened to, supported, informed and safe.

Dr Reena Kotecha is a Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with 22 years of clinical experience and more than 10 years as a Consultant. Her private obstetric practice is based at The Portland Hospital in London, with appointments arranged through ObGyn Matters.

Her obstetric specialisms include complex antenatal care, intrapartum care, twin pregnancies, fetal medicine, obstetric medicine and sonography. She holds a Diploma in Fetal Medicine from King's College London.

Within the NHS, Dr Kotecha has led an Obstetric department and currently serves as lead for twin pregnancies. She is also involved in undergraduate medical student training in the specialty.
